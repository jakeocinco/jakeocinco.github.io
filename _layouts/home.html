Hello, My name is Jake Carlson and I am currently a fourth year at the University of Cincinnati studying to earn my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science.

Co-op Experience

Professionally, I have had two amazing work opportunities at both L3Harris and 84.51. I did my first two semesters of work at L3Harris, where I was thrust into a work environment for the first time and had to learn on the job. As a defense contractor, I was involved in a large scale project developing software that interfaced with a visible and infrared cameras, but most importantly I learned how to operate amongst and lead a team and the riggers of testing and documentation involving large releases.

During my second co-op, this time at 84.51, I was in a very different environment. At 84.51, I was doing web development where my team was tasked with creating a web API as well as a front-end web application that uses it. This involved learning a lot of new frameworks such as Angular and Spring, and even more new concepts and ideas. Luckily, I was able to spend an extended amount of time there and worked for eight consecutive months, which allowed me to really embed myself into the team. On a not as lucky note, due to COVID-19, a good portion of this time was spent working from home which forced me to quickly adapt to a very new environment once again.
